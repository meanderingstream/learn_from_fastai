Title: What can you learn from the Fast.ai MOOC?

We’ll cover a quick flyby of the concepts taught in the free course and provide some useful insights and supplemental resources.

Developer?  Fast.ai breaks several myths about Deep Learning.  You don’t have to have a PhD to utilize Deep Learning.  With a year of development experience and if you are a self-starter, you can grow into a Machine Learning practitioner using the concepts taught in Part 1 and Part 2.

Not a Developer?  Included in Fast.ai is a section on deep learning using structured data.  Structured data is the combination of continuous and categorical values.  Many enterprises have a trove of categorical and numerical data where Deep Learning could be used.  Come learn about how Deep Learning can be applied on typical enterprise data.
